### Added — content

- **Onsite kickoff option (SOW Section 1).** Hasen may host the kickoff workshop onsite at Hasen's Fort Worth office or virtually via Microsoft Teams, at Hasen's choice. ITECS offers the onsite option at no additional travel charge. The onsite workshop is approximately 3-4 hours and is positioned as the recommended option for in-person stakeholder alignment, technical environment review, and secure credentials handoff. The virtual option uses the same agenda and outcomes. Section title remains "Kickoff and Access Planning."
- **Engagement Team subsection.** New subsection under About ITECS identifying the named team:
  - Engagement Sponsor and Lead AI Strategist: Brian Desmot, CIO and CISO
  - Technical Account Manager: Jordan Emerle
  - AI Specialist: TBD (assigned at engagement letter execution)
  - Includes a paragraph clarifying Brian's executive sponsor role and Jordan's day-to-day technical account management responsibilities.
- **Client Responsibilities section.** New full section between Phase 1 Assumptions and Out of Scope, formalizing Hasen's eight responsibilities: designate single point of contact, confirm kickoff format, provide source documents, identify pilot users, confirm reviewer availability, purchase Claude Team seats, respond to ITECS within two business days, notify ITECS of scope/stakeholder/timing changes.
- **Phase 1 Acceptance Criteria subsection.** New subsection under Investment, between Payment Terms and Third-Party Subscription Cost. Defines the four conditions that constitute Phase 1 completion and trigger the second 50% invoice: prompt playbook validated against three Work Order scenarios, audit log template operational and used for at least one test cycle, both training sessions delivered and recorded with quick-reference workflow guide accepted, and Phase 1 handoff notes delivered.
- **Phase 1 at a Glance callout panel.** New section directly after the cover page providing a scannable executive overview: Investment, Payment Terms, Implementation Duration, Optimization Window, Initial Users, AI Platform, Kickoff Format, Training Format. Designed to give stakeholders a 60-second snapshot before they engage with the full proposal.
