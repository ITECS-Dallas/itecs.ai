## Our Approach to AI Pricing

ITECS empowers SMB and mid-market organizations to adopt AI confidently — with predictable pricing, productized engagement options, and the right platform fit for your business. We work across all major commercial AI platforms — **Anthropic Claude, OpenAI ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, GitHub Copilot, and others** — and recommend what fits your needs rather than locking you into a single vendor.

Most of our clients begin with a flat-fee productized engagement, then graduate into either custom development or ongoing managed AI services. Hourly rates are published below for engagements that fall outside the productized offerings.
